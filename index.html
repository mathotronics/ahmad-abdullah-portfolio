About Ahmad

Math teacher and STEM educator focused on making learning clear, practical, and engaging for students through Arduino, robotics, and project-based learning.

Mathematics Arduino STEM Wedo 2.0 Curriculum Design

I am Ahmad Abdullah, a STEM Education Specialist and Mathematics Teacher with a passion for combining engineering, coding, electronics, robotics, and mathematics into meaningful classroom experiences. I believe students learn deeply when they build, test, discuss, and improve real projects.

My teaching approach is structured, student-centered, and practical. I create lessons that move from clear explanations to guided practice and then to hands-on application. Through Arduino projects, Wedo 2.0 robotics activities, STEM challenges, and math-based investigations, I help students connect what they learn to real-world systems.

Through Math-O-Tronics, I develop classroom-ready resources for teachers who want to bring interactive STEM learning into their classrooms without losing clarity, organization, or curriculum focus.
